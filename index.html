Metcar, Inc. is recognized as one of the pioneers in the use of anthracite coal in the steelmaking industry. We have been providing carbon products to the American Steel Industry since 1981, becoming a valuable source of charge carbon and foamy slag carbon.

Metcar is a service oriented company that will work with customers to provide a custom program of material and equipment to keep pace with changing technology and meet customer needs based on sizing and usage.
